Organising the event is very challenging when you are not an event planner. But the biggest 2 challenges are getting everyone to RSVP on time and getting the right guest speakers. We started with venue costs, asking many venues for a bunch of stuff that we'd need on the day. And found the best price plus ambience. Then we moved on to march quotes. Deciding what merchandise we want and getting costs. Then ran a competition for artwork for the shirt and hoodies we planned to make. We then asked a bunch of external people if they would be interested in speaking and shortlisted the right people and topics who responded as being available. We then asked internally for speakers. After we had a list of speakers, we then met regularly to check on the status of the out standing items, and to fi out agenda for the day. And the week before was finalising, confirming and executing the event. Afterwards we also sent out a feedback form to our company to get positive and negative feedback to improve the next event.
